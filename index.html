Extras?

Yep, this version of concrete includes a collection of extras that fill in a few gaps missing from the original.

Note that many of these extras abandon the classless nature of the original.

A simple CSS-grid based grid from SmolCSS.

Boxed

A border around a value, or something that you'd like to highlight.

It adds oooomph.

Also available as .boxed-dashed

Inputs

Unfortunately, there's a few missing inputs in the original.

Using .centered on a parent results in a centered child.
